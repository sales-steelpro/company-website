What we stock

Product range

Comprehensive inventory including structural steel, steel plates, coils, bars, and specialty alloys for diverse industrial applications.

Universal beams (UB)

Hot‑rolled I‑sections for primary structural framing, 150UB to 610UB.

Universal columns (UC)

Wide‑flange columns for vertical load applications, 100UC to 310UC.

Parallel flange channels

PFC sections for purlins, lintels and framing, 75PFC to 380PFC.

Welded beams & columns

Heavy WB/WC sections for high‑load industrial and infrastructure work.

RHS / SHS / CHS

Hollow structural sections in painted, galvanised and raw finishes.

Angles & flats

Equal and unequal angles, flat bar in standard and custom lengths.

Structural plate

Grade 250/350 plate from 3 mm to 100 mm, cut‑to‑size available.

Wear plate

400/450/500 HB abrasion‑resistant plate for mining and earthmoving.

Hot rolled coil

HRC in commercial and structural grades, slit and sheared to order.

Cold rolled coil

CRC for precision forming, roll‑forming and manufacturing lines.

Galvanised coil & sheet

Zinc‑coated coil and sheet for corrosion‑critical applications.

Checker / floor plate

Raised‑pattern plate for flooring, walkways and tray bodies.

Round bar

Bright and black round bar in carbon and alloy grades.

Square & flat bar

Merchant bar range for fabrication and general engineering.

Deformed reinforcing bar

Rebar to AS/NZS 4671 in standard stock and cut‑and‑bend.

Bright steel shafting

Precision‑ground shafting for machining and drive applications.

Rail & crane rail

Standard and crane rail profiles for industrial track work.

Custom sections

Rolled or fabricated sections to drawing, by indent order.

Quenched & tempered

High‑strength Q&T plate (700 MPa+) for structural and defence work.

Weathering steel

Corten‑type grades for architectural and exposed structures.

Boiler & pressure vessel

PV‑grade plate with full certification for pressure equipment.

Stainless steel

304/316 plate, sheet, bar and hollow sections.

Alloy & tool steel

4140, 4340, EN series and tool steels for engineering applications.

Indent & special order

Hard‑to‑source grades imported to specification on request.

How we work

From mill to site.

  1. Enquiry & spec

    Send us your bill of materials or drawings. We confirm grades, sizes, tolerances and certification requirements.

  2. Source & secure

    We quote from stock or source mill‑direct from our vetted international supply network at competitive rates.

  3. Inspect & certify

    Every consignment is checked against order and standard, with mill certificates supplied for full traceability.

  4. Deliver on schedule

    Flexible delivery — single drop or staged call‑offs timed to your program, anywhere in Australia.
